Privacy Policy

BeepBridge — developed by Diego Cancela (mail@diegocancela.cc)

Data collection

BeepBridge does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Camera usage

The app uses the device camera solely to scan barcodes and QR codes. No images or video are stored or transmitted beyond the scanning process.

Scanned data

Scanned values are sent only to the server URL configured by the user on their local network. No data is sent to any external server operated by the developer.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's privacy policy.

Users can remove ads via an in-app purchase.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact mail@diegocancela.cc.
