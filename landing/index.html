Your phone. Your barcode scanner.

Scan any barcode or QR code with your phone and instantly type it into any app on your desktop. No drivers. No cables.

Download for Windows Get it on Google Play

How it works

1

Run the server

Start BeepBridge on your desktop. It listens for scans on your local network.

2

Connect the app

Open the Android app and enter your computer's local IP address.

3

Scan — it types

Point your phone at any barcode. The value appears on your desktop, typed automatically.
